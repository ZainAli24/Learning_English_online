## Application for Bad Sewerage System:

To
Islamabad Sewerage Authority

Subject: Bad Sewerage System in our Area

Respected Authority,
I am resident of G11 in Islamabad. The sewerage system of this area is very bad. Here are the common problems that the people of this area are facing: Dirty water is accumulated on the road. The reason is that there is no proper drainage system in our area and the area is filled with this dirty water with mosquitoes and germs. The result of this is very bad smell in our area which affects the health of children and one more problem is that people of this area are facing difficulty while walking on the road because of dirty water and not proper sewerage system.

Please solve this problem and make a proper sewerage system in our area and clean our roads. The people of this area are very sad and many people’s health are affected because of this.

We are very thankful to you.
Zain Ali

# 📘 1. Formal Application Structure (Yaad rakho)

Professional application mein 5 parts hote hain:

### 1. To (authority)

👉 kisko likh rahe ho

### 2. Subject

👉 masla kya hai (short line)

### 3. Introduction

👉 tum kaun ho + area

### 4. Problem description

👉 detail mein issue

### 5. Request + ending

👉 solution ki request + thanks

---

# 🧠 2. Tumhari application ko improve karne ka tareeqa

Main tumhe batata hoon **kahan kya change hota hai:**

---

## ✨ (A) Language Formal karo

❌ "very bad"
👉 ✅ "poor condition / unsatisfactory condition"

---

❌ "dirty water accumulated"
👉 ✅ "wastewater is accumulated"

---

❌ "people facing difficulty"
👉 ✅ "residents are facing difficulties"

---

## ✨ (B) Sentence structure clean karo

👉 Long sentence ko tod do
👉 Simple + formal English use karo

---

## ✨ (C) Professional words use karo

Use these words:

* problem → issue
* bad → poor / severe
* fix → resolve / address
* people → residents
* area → locality

---

# 🔥 3. Example transformation (tumhara style → formal style)

### ❌ Simple:

"The sewerage system is very bad"

### ✅ Formal:

"The sewerage system in our area is in a very poor condition"

---

### ❌ Simple:

"Dirty water is on road"

### ✅ Formal:

"Wastewater is accumulated on the roads"

---

### ❌ Simple:

"Please solve this problem"

### ✅ Formal:

"You are kindly requested to resolve this issue at the earliest"

---

# 🧠 4. Final Formula (yaad rakhna)

👉 Formal Application =

**Polite + Clear + Structured + Professional Words**
