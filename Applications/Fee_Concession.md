## *🧠 Application Structure:*

👉 Application mein 5 parts hote hain:

1. **To** (kisko likh rahe ho)
2. **Subject** (kis bare mein hai)
3. **Introduction** (tum kaun ho)
4. **Problem** (fee issue)
5. **Request** (concession mangna)

---
