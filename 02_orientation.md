## **After That vs After it:**

"After that" aur "After it" dono ka matlab aksar ek hi hota hai—"uske baad"—lekin inke istemaal (usage) mein thora farq hai. 

Yahan roman urdu mein inka tafseeli farq samjhaya gaya hai:

### 1. After That (Zyaada Common aur Standard) 
- Meaning: Uske baad / Uske nateejay mein.

- Usage: Yeh aam taur par sequence of events (kisi kaam ke foran baad doosra kaam) dikhane ke liye istemaal hota hai. Yeh kisse ya kahani sunate waqt behtar lagta hai.

- Example: "First I had breakfast, and **after that** I went to work." (Pehle maine nashta kiya, aur uske baad kaam par chala gaya). 

### 2. After It (Specific aur Noun-based)
- Meaning: Uske baad (kisi khaas cheez ya waqt ke).

- Usage: 'It' aik pronoun hai, isliye 'After it' aksar kisi specific cheez (noun) ko refer karta hai jo pichli baat mein zikr hui ho.

- Example: "I fixed the chair. After it, the room looked better." (Maine kursi theek ki. Uske baad [us kursi ke theek hone ke baad], kamra behtar lag raha tha).


### (Key Differences)

| Feature | After That | After It |
|---------|-----------|---------|
| Istemal | General/Sequence (kisi event ke baad) | Specific (kisi khaas cheez ke baad) |
| Pronoun | 'That' har tarah ki baaton/events ke liye aa sakta hai. | 'It' sirf kisi aik specific noun ke liye aata hai. |
| Feel | Kahani ya steps batane mein natural lagta hai. | Thora robotic ya bohot specific lag sakta hai. |


**Bottom Line**: Aam bol-chaal mein "After that" ya "Afterwards" (uske baad) ka istemaal zyaada behtar aur natural samjha jata hai. 


-----------------

### Use of To:
"To" ka Istemal: Jab bhi koi kaam karne ka irada ho (e.g., khelna, parhna), toh "to" lagayein (To play, To study, To start).

## 📘 Noun kya hota hai?

👉 **Noun = kisi bhi cheez ka naam**

Yani:

* insan ka naam
* jagah ka naam
* cheez ka naam
* jaanwar ka naam

---

### 🧠 Easy samajh:

👉 **Jiska naam ho = Noun**

---

### 📌 Examples:

#### 👤 Insaan (Person)

* Ali
* Sara
* teacher

#### 📍 Jagah (Place)

* school
* Pakistan
* room

#### 📦 Cheez (Thing)

* book
* mobile
* chair

#### 🐶 Jaanwar (Animal)

* cat
* dog

---

### 🧾 Simple Sentence Example

* Ali is a boy.
  👉 **Ali (noun)**
  👉 **boy (noun)**

* I have a book.
  👉 **book (noun)**

---

#### 🔥 Short Rule

👉 **Name = Noun**

---

<br>



## 📘 Types of Noun (Asaan Tarah)

### 🟢 1. Proper Noun (Khaas Naam)

👉 Kisi **special insan, jagah ya cheez ka naam**

📌 Rule:

* First letter **capital** hota hai

#### Examples:

* Ali
* Pakistan
* Lahore
* Monday

👉 Sentence:

* Ali is my friend.
* I live in Pakistan.

---

### 🔵 2. Common Noun (Aam Naam)

👉 General naam (koi bhi ho sakta hai)

#### Examples:

* boy
* city
* country
* teacher

👉 Sentence:

* He is a boy.
* I live in a city.

---

### 🟡 3. Collective Noun (Group ka naam)

👉 Jab **group ko ek naam diya jaye**

#### Examples:

* team
* family
* class
* group

👉 Sentence:

* My family is happy.
* The team is playing.

---

### 🟣 4. Material Noun (Cheez kis se bani hai)

👉 Wo cheez jisse kuch banaya jata hai

#### Examples:

* gold
* water
* milk
* wood

👉 Sentence:

* This ring is gold.
* I drink milk.

---

### 🔴 5. Abstract Noun (Jo dikhai nahi deta)

👉 Feelings ya ideas (jo hum dekh nahi sakte)

#### Examples:

- love (pyar)
- happiness (khushi)
- sadness (gham)
- fear (dar)
- anger (gussa)

👉 Sentence:

* I feel happiness.
* Love is important.

---

## 🧠 Easy Trick

| Type       | Matlab      |
| ---------- | ----------- |
| Proper     | Khaas naam  |
| Common     | Aam naam    |
| Collective | Group       |
| Material   | Kis se bana |
| Abstract   | Feelings    |

---

### 🔥 Quick Example (Sab ek sath)

👉 Ali (Proper) is a boy (Common).
👉 His family (Collective) is full of love (Abstract).
👉 He drinks milk (Material).

## 📘 Pronoun kya hota hai?

👉 **Pronoun = noun ki jagah use hota hai**

---

### 🧠 Simple samajh:

👉 Bar bar naam repeat na karne ke liye
👉 hum **pronoun** use karte hain

---

### 📌 Example:

#### ❌ Without Pronoun:

Ali is a boy. **Ali** is playing. **Ali** is happy.
👉 (bar bar Ali aa raha hai ❌)

#### ✅ With Pronoun:

Ali is a boy. **He** is playing. **He** is happy.
👉 (easy aur short 👍)

---

### 😊 Common Pronouns

* I (main)
* You (tum)
* He (woh - larka)
* She (woh - larki)
* It (cheez/janwar)
* We (hum)
* They (woh log)

---

### 🧾 Simple Sentences

* I am a student.
* He is my friend.
* She is happy.
* They are playing.

---

### 🔥 Easy Rule

👉 **Noun repeat na karna ho → Pronoun use karo**

---

### ⚡ Short Trick

👉 **Name ki jagah jo aaye = Pronoun**
