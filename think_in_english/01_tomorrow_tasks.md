### 2. Use of `To`:
Great 👍 ye bohat important concept hai — tum sahi soch rahe ho 👌

---

# 📘 “to” ka confusion clear karte hain

👉 **“to” ka hamesha matlab “par” nahi hota** ❌
👉 “to” ke **2 main uses** hote hain

---

# 🟢 1. “to” = direction (taraf / par)

👉 jab kisi jagah ki taraf jana ho

* I go **to** school
  👉 main school **jata hoon**

👉 yahan “to” = **ki taraf**

---

# 🔵 2. “to” + verb = purpose (kaam karne ke liye)

👉 jab “to” ke baad verb aaye
👉 iska matlab hota hai: **“karne ke liye”**

---

# 🔥 Examples

### 👉 to attend

* I go to university **to attend class**
  👉 main university jata hoon **class attend karne ke liye**

---

### 👉 to sleep

* I go to bed **to sleep**
  👉 main bistar par jata hoon **sone ke liye**

---

# 🧠 Easy Trick

👉 **to + place** → taraf / par
👉 **to + verb** → karne ke liye

---

# ⚡ Compare

* I go **to school** → jagah
* I go **to study** → kaam (study karne ke liye)

---

# 🎯 Final Simple Line

👉 **to + verb = karne ke liye**
