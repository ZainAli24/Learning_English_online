
### **Topic: Technology has changed our lifestyle.**

#### **1. Opening Sentence (Your Opinion)**

**There is no doubt that** technology makes life easier, and it has completely transformed our lifestyle.

#### **2. Reasons (Supporting the Opinion)**

**In my opinion**, there are many main advantages of technology that prove how it makes our lives better and more efficient:

* **In education:** Technology transformed education completely. Whatever we want to learn, we can learn online through the internet. There are many apps, video tutorials, and AI tutors available on the internet, and we can learn from anywhere in the world. Also, technology like AI helps teachers to organize lessons, track student progress, and personalize learning for every student. Teachers can automate the evaluation process through AI—AI analyzes and assigns a grade to every student based on their marks. Also, students can use AI for learning new things, making presentations, summarizing notes, and getting important key points from video lectures. Additionally, students can use AI for English learning, where they can practice speaking with AI.
* **In communication:** **From my point of view**, we can call, text, or video chat with anyone around the world through mobile phones. Imagine you are living far away from your family—with just one video call, you feel like you are right there in between your family. Also, technology keeps us updated; we know what is happening in the world in just a second.
* **In healthcare:** **It is widely believed that** technology like AI acts like a second pair of eyes for X-rays and MRI scans, quickly spotting tumors or broken bones that humans might miss. Instead of a doctor typing on a computer during your visit, AI listens to the conversation and writes the medical notes automatically. AI agents listen to the conversation between a doctor and a patient and automatically write down the official medical report. It can also analyze a patient's DNA structure to see which diseases they might get in the future, or exactly which medicine will work best for their body.

#### **3. Examples (Real-World Proof)**

To support these points, we can look at how technology and AI are driving the modern world through powerful real-world examples:

* **Autonomous Driving:** Technology like AI is taking away many jobs. AI and robots are replacing many jobs, such as autopilot (self-driving) cars. In these cars, AI agents are used like a brain, and these cars make decisions automatically. Fully autonomous, driverless cars are operating in specific metropolitan areas. In the US, these self-driving cars are used in cities such as San Francisco, Los Angeles, Phoenix, and Austin. In China, autonomous taxis operate in cities like Beijing, Shanghai, and Wuhan.
* **Workplace Efficiency:** Also, an AI employee works much smarter and more efficiently than human employees—an AI employee works 168 hours a week, whereas a human employee works 40 hours a week, which means the AI employee works 24/7.

#### **4. Counter-Perspective & Critical Analysis (Disadvantages)**

However, **to some extent**, technology is not always used wisely. Pollution caused by industries, deforestation, and climate change are all side effects of technology. One most important disadvantage is overreliance, where some students depend too much on AI tools for quick online answers instead of developing their own critical thinking.

#### **5. Conclusion**
People fear that AI is replacing human jobs.
Therefore, **I strongly believe that** people need to learn new skills to keep up with these changes, so those people who keep up with these changes will not be replaced.
