# At use :

English grammar mei preposition **'At'** ka istemaal aam taur par **khususi waqt (precise time)**, **khaas jagah (specific location)**, ya **jab kisi cheez par focus** karna ho, tab kiya jaata hai. Urdu/Roman Urdu mein iska matlab **"par"** ya **"me"** hota hai. 


Yahan 'At' ke istemaal ke khaas mauqe hain:

### 1. Khaas Waqt Batane Ke Liye (Precise Time)
Jab aap ko bilkul sahi time batana ho, to 'at' use karte hain. 

- Examples:
  - I will meet you at 5 PM. (Main tumse 5 baje milunga.)
  - The meeting is at noon. (Meeting dopahar mein hai.)
  - He came at lunchtime. (Woh khane ke waqt aaya.) 

### 2. Khaas Jagah ya Point Batane Ke Liye (Specific Place/Location)
Jab aap kisi specific jagah par hote hain (na ke uske andar), tab 'at' lagate hain.

- Examples:
   - She is waiting at the bus stop. (Woh bus stop par intezaar kar rahi hai.)
   - I am at home. (Main ghar par hoon.)
   - He is at the door. (Woh darwaze par hai.) 

### 3. Kaam Karne Wali Jagah Par (Workplace/School)
Jab aap apne kaam ya school par hote hain.

- Examples:
  -  She is at school. (Woh school mein hai.)
  -  I am at work. (Main kaam par hoon.) 

### 4. Jab Kisi Cheez ki Taraf Ishara ya Focus Ho (Target/Focus)
Jab aap kisi ko kuch dikha rahe hon ya kisi cheez par ghaur kar rahe hon.

- Examples:
  - Look at the whiteboard. (Whiteboard ki taraf dekho.)
  - She smiled at him. (Woh us par muskurayi.) 

### 5. Kuch Khaas Phrases mein:
- At the weekend / At weekends (Haftay ke akhir mein)
- At night (Raat mein)
- At Christmas / At Eid (Kisi festival par) 

### Short Summary Table:

| Context   | Usage                  | Example        |
|-----------|------------------------|----------------|
| Time      | 5:00, Noon, Night      | At 6 o'clock   |
| Place     | Bus stop, Door, Corner | At the gate    |
| Work      | School, Office, Home   | At the office  |
| Direction | Looking, Laughing      | Look at me     |


**Note:** "In" bade areas (in Delhi) ke liye use hota hai, aur "At" chote ya specific points (at the shop) ke liye

# 📘 Is / Are (Super Simple Rule)

### 🟢 1. "IS" kab use hota hai?

👉 Jab **1 cheez / 1 banda** ho (single)

Use with:

* He
* She
* It
* 1 name (Ali, Sara)

📌 Urdu: **"hai"**

#### Examples:

* He is a boy. → woh larka **hai**
* She is happy. → woh khush **hai**
* Ali is eating. → Ali kha raha **hai**

---

### 🔵 2. "ARE" kab use hota hai?

👉 Jab **1 se zyada (many)** ho

Use with:

* You
* We
* They
* plural cheezen (books, cars)

📌 Urdu: **"hain / ho"**

#### Examples:

* You are a student. → tum student **ho**
* We are friends. → hum dost **hain**
* They are playing. → woh khel rahe **hain**

---

### 🧠 Easy Trick (Yaad rakhne ka tareeqa)

👉 **1 = IS**
👉 **Many = ARE**

---

### ⭐ Special Rule (Important)

👉 **"I" ke sath:**

* ❌ I is
* ❌ I are
* ✅ **I am**

Example:

* I am happy. → main khush hoon

---

### 🟡 Bonus (There is / There are)

👉 Pehle dekho baad mein kya hai:

* **1 cheez → is**

  * There is a cat. → ek billi hai

* **zyada cheezen → are**

  * There are cats. → billiiyan hain

---

### 🔥 Short Summary

| Subject                | Use     |
| ---------------------- | ------- |
| He / She / It / 1 name | **is**  |
| You / We / They / many | **are** |
| I                      | **am**  |

# 📘 Subject, Verb, Object (Simple Notes)

### 1. English Example

**Sentence:** She reads a book.

* **Subject (Doer):** She → jo kaam kar rahi hai
* **Verb (Action):** reads → kaam (parhna)
* **Object (Receiver):** a book → jis par kaam ho raha hai

👉 Structure:
**Subject + Verb + Object (SVO)**

---

### 2. Urdu Example

**Sentence:** Aslam football khelta hai.

* **Subject (Fa'il):** Aslam → jo kaam kar raha hai
* **Verb (Fail):** khelta hai → kaam
* **Object (Mafool):** football → jis par kaam ho raha hai

👉 Structure:
**Subject + Object + Verb (SOV)**

---

### 🔥 Key Difference (Asaan Samajh)

| Feature          | Subject (Fa'il) | Object (Mafool)       |
| ---------------- | --------------- | --------------------- |
| Kaam             | Kaam karne wala | Jis par kaam hota hai |
| English Position | Start mein      | Verb ke baad          |
| Urdu Position    | Start mein      | Verb se pehle         |

---

### 🧠 Sentence Structure Summary

* **English:**
  👉 Subject + Verb + Object
  👉 Example: **Ali eats apple.**

* **Urdu:**
  👉 Subject + Object + Verb
  👉 Example: **Ali saib khata hai.**
