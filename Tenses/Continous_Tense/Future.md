## Pehchaan (Identification)

Jab Urdu mein sentence ke aakhir mein yeh alfaaz aayein:

**raha hoga, rahi hogi, rahay hongay, raha hounga, rahi houngi**

Toh woh **Future Continuous Tense** hota hai.

Jaise:

- Mein khana **kha raha hounga** → I will be eating food.
- Woh school **ja rahi hogi** → She will be going to school.
- Hum cricket **khel rahay hongay** → We will be playing cricket.

## Structure

**Positive:** Subject + will be + V1+ing + Object

Sab subjects ke saath **will be** lagta hai — koi change nahi!

**Negative:** Subject + will not be + V1+ing + Object
- Mein nahi ja **raha hounga** → I will not be going.

**Question:** Will + Subject + be + V1+ing?
- Kya tum parh **rahay hoge**? → Will you be studying?

## Teeno Continuous Ka Farq 🧠

| Urdu mein aakhir | Tense | English |
|---|---|---|
| raha **hai**, rahi **hai** | Present Continuous | is/am/are + V1+ing |
| raha **tha**, rahi **thi** | Past Continuous | was/were + V1+ing |
| raha **hoga**, rahi **hogi** | Future Continuous | will be + V1+ing |

---

<br>



### **Rule**: **Negative** = anything, nothing. **Positive** = something.
