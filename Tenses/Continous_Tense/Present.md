## Pehchaan (Identification)

Jab Urdu mein sentence ke aakhir mein yeh alfaaz aayein:

**raha hai, rahi hai, rahay hain, raha hoon, rahi hoon**

Toh woh **Present Continuous Tense** hota hai.

Jaise:

- Mein khana **kha raha hoon** → I am eating food.
- Woh school **ja raha hai** → He is going to school.
- Woh khana **bana rahi hai** → She is cooking food.
- Hum cricket **khel rahay hain** → We are playing cricket.

## Structure

**Positive:** Subject + is/am/are + V1+ing + Object

| Subject | Helping Verb |
|---|---|
| I | **am** |
| He / She / It | **is** |
| You / We / They | **are** |

**Negative:** Subject + is/am/are + **not** + V1+ing + Object
- Mein nahi ja **raha** → I am not going.
- Woh nahi kha **raha** → He is not eating.

**Question:** Is/Am/Are + Subject + V1+ing + Object?
- Kya tum parh **rahay** ho? → Are you studying?
- Kya woh so **raha** hai? → Is he sleeping?
