
## Pehchaan (Identification)

Jab Urdu mein sentence ke aakhir mein yeh alfaaz aayein:

**raha tha, rahi thi, rahay thay**

Toh woh **Past Continuous Tense** hota hai.

Jaise:

- Mein khana **kha raha tha** → I was eating food.
- Woh school **ja rahi thi** → She was going to school.
- Hum cricket **khel rahay thay** → We were playing cricket.

## Structure

**Positive:** Subject + was/were + V1+ing + Object

| Subject | Helping Verb |
|---|---|
| I / He / She / It | **was** |
| You / We / They | **were** |

**Negative:** Subject + was/were + **not** + V1+ing
- Mein nahi ja **raha tha** → I was not going.

**Question:** Was/Were + Subject + V1+ing?
- Kya tum parh **rahay thay**? → Were you studying?

## Present vs Past Continuous — Farq

| Urdu | Tense | English |
|---|---|---|
| raha **hai**, rahi **hai** | Present Continuous | is/am/are + V1+ing |
| raha **tha**, rahi **thi** | Past Continuous | was/were + V1+ing |
