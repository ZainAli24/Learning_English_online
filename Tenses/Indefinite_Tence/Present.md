# 1. "Indefinite" ka matlab kya hai?

**Indefinite** ka seedha matlab hai **"ghair muayyan"** yani koi kaam jo **aam tor par hota hai**, lekin hum yeh nahi bata rahe ke woh kaam **abhi ho raha hai, ho chuka hai, ya kitni der se ho raha hai**. Bas itna bata rahe hain ke **yeh kaam hota hai, hua, ya hoga.**

Jaise Urdu mein hum kehte hain:
- "Main khana **khata** hoon" — bas aam baat hai, roz ka mamool hai.
- "Main ne khana **khaya**" — ho gaya, khatam.
- "Main khana **khaon ga**" — aage hoga.

Yeh teeno **Indefinite Tenses** hain!

## Asaan trick yaad rakhne ke liye:

Socho ke **Indefinite = Simple**. Bohat se grammar books mein inhe **Simple Tenses** bhi kehte hain. Toh:

- Present Indefinite = Simple Present
- Past Indefinite = Simple Past
- Future Indefinite = Simple Future

**"Simple"** isliye kehte hain kyunke yeh sab se **asaan** aur **seedhi** baat hoti hai — koi extra detail nahi, bas kaam **hota hai, hua, ya hoga.**
