# 1. "Indefinite" ka matlab kya hai?

**Indefinite** ka seedha matlab hai **"ghair muayyan"** yani koi kaam jo **aam tor par hota hai**, lekin hum yeh nahi bata rahe ke woh kaam **abhi ho raha hai, ho chuka hai, ya kitni der se ho raha hai**. Bas itna bata rahe hain ke **yeh kaam hota hai, hua, ya hoga.**

Jaise Urdu mein hum kehte hain:
- "Main khana **khata** hoon" — bas aam baat hai, roz ka mamool hai.
- "Main ne khana **khaya**" — ho gaya, khatam.
- "Main khana **khaon ga**" — aage hoga.

Yeh teeno **Indefinite Tenses** hain!

## Asaan trick yaad rakhne ke liye:

Socho ke **Indefinite = Simple**. Bohat se grammar books mein inhe **Simple Tenses** bhi kehte hain. Toh:

- Present Indefinite = Simple Present
- Past Indefinite = Simple Past
- Future Indefinite = Simple Future

**"Simple"** isliye kehte hain kyunke yeh sab se **asaan** aur **seedhi** baat hoti hai — koi extra detail nahi, bas kaam **hota hai, hua, ya hoga.**

### Improvements:
- He/She/It ke saath hamesha s/es lagao (prays, boils, cooks)
- DOES ke saath hamesha V1 — yeh aapko pata hai lekin ek jagah bhool gaye
- **"es" kab lagti hai — sirf ch, sh, ss, x, o ke baad. Baqi sab pe sirf "s"**

# Present Indfinit : 
Bilkul! Chalo **Present Indefinite Tense** ki pehchaan bhi seekhte hain! 🎯

## Pehchaan (Identification)

Jab Urdu mein sentence ke aakhir mein yeh alfaaz aayein:

**ta hai, ti hai, tay hain, ta hoon, ti hoon**

Toh woh **Present Indefinite Tense** hota hai.

Jaise:

- Mein khana **khata hoon** → I eat food.
- Woh school **jaata hai** → He goes to school.
- Woh khana **banati hai** → She cooks food.
- Hum cricket **kheltay hain** → We play cricket.

## Structure

**Positive:** Subject + V1 + Object
- He/She/It ke saath → V1 mein **s/es** lagao
- I/You/We/They ke saath → simple V1

**Negative:** Subject + do not / does not + V1
- Mein nahi **jaata** → I do not go.
- Woh nahi **jaata** → He does not go.

**Question:** Do / Does + Subject + V1?
- Kya tum **jaate** ho? → Do you go?
- Kya woh **jaata** hai? → Does he go?

## Asaan Trick 🧠

| Urdu mein aakhir | English Tense |
|---|---|
| ta hai, ti hai, tay hain | Present Indefinite |
| tha, thi, thay | Past Indefinite |
