Bilkul! Chalo ab **Future Indefinite Tense** ki pehchaan seekhte hain! 🎯

## Pehchaan (Identification)

Jab Urdu mein sentence ke aakhir mein yeh alfaaz aayein:

**ga, gi, ge, gaa, gee, gen**

Toh woh **Future Indefinite Tense** hota hai.

Jaise:

- Mein khana **khaun ga** → I will eat food.
- Woh school **jaye gi** → She will go to school.
- Hum cricket **khelein ge** → We will play cricket.
- Woh kal **aaye ga** → He will come tomorrow.

## Structure

**Positive:** Subject + will + V1 + Object
- Mein kal padhunga → I will study tomorrow.
- Woh ghar aayega → He will come home.

**Negative:** Subject + will not (won't) + V1 + Object
- Mein nahi jaunga → I will not go.
- Woh nahi aayegi → She will not come.

**Question:** Will + Subject + V1 + Object?
- Kya tum kal aao ge? → Will you come tomorrow?
- Kya woh khana banaye gi? → Will she cook food?

## Zaroori Rule ⚠️

Future Indefinite mein hamesha **will + V1** lagta hai — chahe subject koi bhi ho. Yahan "s/es" ya "did" kuch nahi lagta, bas **will** kaam karta hai!

## Asaan Trick — Teeno Tenses Ki Pehchaan 🧠

| Urdu mein aakhir | English Tense | Structure |
|---|---|---|
| ta hai, ti hai, tay hain | Present Indefinite | V1 (s/es with he/she/it) |
| tha, thi, thay | Past Indefinite | V2 |
| ga, gi, ge | Future Indefinite | will + V1 |
