# **Past Perfect Tense:**

## Pehchaan (Identification)

Jab Urdu mein sentence ke aakhir mein yeh alfaaz aayein:

**chuka tha, chuki thi, chukey thay, liya tha, diya tha, kiya tha, gaya tha, gayi thi, gaye thay**

Toh woh **Past Perfect Tense** hota hai.

Jaise:

- Mein khana kha **chuka tha** → I had eaten food.
- Woh school ja **chuka tha** → He had gone to school.
- Woh khana bana **chuki thi** → She had cooked food.
- Hum kaam kar **chukey thay** → We had finished work.

## Structure

**Positive:** Subject + had + V3 + Object

Sab subjects ke saath **had** lagta hai — koi change nahi! Yeh Present Perfect se easy hai kyunki has/have ka confusion nahi.

**Negative:** Subject + had + **not** + V3 + Object
- Mein nahi kha **chuka tha** → I had not eaten.

**Question:** Had + Subject + V3 + Object?
- Kya tum ne khana kha **liya tha**? → Had you eaten food?

## Present Perfect vs Past Perfect — Farq

| Urdu | Tense | English |
|---|---|---|
| kha **chuka hai** | Present Perfect | I **have** eaten |
| kha **chuka tha** | Past Perfect | I **had** eaten |

Asaan trick: **"hai" → have/has** aur **"tha" → had**

## Kab Use Hota Hai? 🧠

Past Perfect tab use hota hai jab **do past actions** mein se **pehle wali action** batani ho:

- Jab mein ghar **pahuncha**, woh ja **chuka tha**.
→ When I reached home, he **had gone**.

Pehle woh gaya (Past Perfect) → phir mein pahuncha (Past Indefinite)
