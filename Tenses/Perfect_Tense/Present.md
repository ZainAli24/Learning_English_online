## **Present Perfect Tense:**
---

## Pehchaan (Identification)

Jab Urdu mein sentence ke aakhir mein yeh alfaaz aayein:

**chuka hai, chuki hai, chukey hain, chuka hoon, liya hai, diya hai, kiya hai, gaya hai, gayi hai, gaye hain**

Toh woh **Present Perfect Tense** hota hai.

Jaise:

- Mein khana kha **chuka hoon** → I have eaten food.
- Woh school ja **chuka hai** → He has gone to school.
- Woh khana bana **chuki hai** → She has cooked food.
- Hum kaam kar **chukey hain** → We have finished work.
- Us ne mujhe phone **kiya hai** → He has called me.

## Structure

**Positive:** Subject + has/have + V3 (third form of verb) + Object

| Subject | Helping Verb |
|---|---|
| I / You / We / They | **have** |
| He / She / It | **has** |

**Negative:** Subject + has/have + **not** + V3 + Object
- Mein nahi kha **chuka** → I have not eaten.
- Woh nahi aaya → He has not come.

**Question:** Has/Have + Subject + V3 + Object?
- Kya tum ne khana kha **liya hai**? → Have you eaten food?
- Kya woh aa **chuka hai**? → Has he come?

## Zaroori Rule ⚠️

Yahan **V3 (past participle)** lagti hai — V2 nahi! Kuch verbs mein V2 aur V3 same hoti hai, kuch mein different:

| V1 | V2 | V3 |
|---|---|---|
| go | went | **gone** |
| eat | ate | **eaten** |
| come | came | **come** |
| do | did | **done** |
| write | wrote | **written** |
| give | gave | **given** |
| take | took | **taken** |
| see | saw | **seen** |
| buy | bought | **bought** (same) |
| make | made | **made** (same) |
