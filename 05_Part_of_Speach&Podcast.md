| ------- | ------ | -------------- |

👉 **Possessive = kisi ki cheez** (my, your, his)

---

* He has a book. → **his book**
* This is my pen. → This pen is **mine**

# 2. 📘 Adjective kya hota hai?

👉 **Adjective = noun ya pronoun ki quality batata hai**

---

# 🧠 Simple samajh:

👉 Adjective batata hai:

* cheez **kesi hai**
* banda **kesa hai**

---

# 🍰 Easy Example (tumhara cake wala 👍)

* simple cake
* decorated cake

👉 yahan:

* **cake = noun**
* **simple / decorated = adjective**

👉 kyun?
👉 kyunki ye cake ki **quality** bata rahe hain

---

# 📌 Aur examples:

* a **big** house → house kesa hai? **big**
* a **happy** boy → boy kesa hai? **happy**
* a **red** car → car kesa hai? **red**

---

# 🔥 Important Point

❌ Adjective sirf “acha” nahi hota
👉 ye **achi + buri dono qualities** batata hai

### Examples:

* good boy ✅
* bad boy ✅
  👉 dono adjective hain

---

# 🧾 Sentence Examples

* He is **happy**.
* She has a **beautiful** dress.
* It is a **small** room.

---

# ⚡ Easy Trick

👉 Question poocho: **“kesa?”**

* house kesa? → big
* boy kesa? → happy

👉 jo answer aaye → **adjective**

---

# 💡 Final Simple Line

👉 **Adjective = kisi cheez ki quality (kesi hai)**

# 3. 📘 Adverb kya hota hai?

👉 **Adverb = verb (kaam) ki quality batata hai**

---

# 🧠 Simple samajh:

👉 Adjective batata hai: **kesa? (noun)**
👉 Adverb batata hai: **kaise? (verb/action)**

---

# ⚡ Easy Example

* He runs.
  👉 bas kaam ho raha hai

* He runs **fast**.
  👉 **kaise run kar raha hai? → fast**

👉 **fast = adverb**

---

# 📌 Aur Examples

* She speaks **slowly**.
  👉 kaise bolti hai? → slowly

* He works **hard**.
  👉 kaise kaam karta hai? → hard

* They are playing **happily**.
  👉 kaise khel rahe hain? → happily

---

# 🔥 Important Point

👉 Adverb aksar **-ly** se end hota hai

* slow → slowly
* happy → happily
* quick → quickly

---

# 🧾 Compare (Adjective vs Adverb)

* He is a **fast** boy. (adjective → boy kesa hai)
* He runs **fast**. (adverb → kaise run karta hai)

---

# 💡 Aur examples

* She sings **beautifully**
* He drives **carefully**
* I speak **clearly**

---

# 🧠 Easy Trick

👉 Question poocho: **“kaise?”**

* run kaise? → fast
* speak kaise? → slowly

👉 Answer = **adverb**

---

# 🎯 Final Simple Line

👉 **Adverb = kaam kaise ho raha hai**

---

# 4. 📘 Conjunctions kya hote hain?

👉 **Conjunction = bridge (pul)**
👉 Ye **do words, do sentences ya ideas ko jorne ka kaam karta hai**

---

# 🧠 Simple samajh:

👉 Jab 2 baatein ek sath jorni hon
👉 hum **conjunction** use karte hain

---

# 🔗 Easy Example:

* I like tea. I like coffee. ❌ (separate sentences)

👉 Conjunction use karo:

* I like tea **and** coffee. ✅

👉 **and = bridge**

---

# 📌 Common Conjunctions

## 1. AND (aur)

👉 same ideas jorne ke liye

* I like tea **and** coffee.
* He is smart **and** kind.

---

## 2. BUT (lekin)

👉 opposite idea

* I am tired **but** I am working.
* He is poor **but** happy.

---

## 3. OR (ya)

👉 choice ke liye

* Do you want tea **or** coffee?
* You can go **or** stay.

---

## 4. BECAUSE (kyun ke)

👉 reason batane ke liye

* I am happy **because** I passed.
* He is sad **because** he is sick.

---

## 5. SO (is liye)

👉 result batane ke liye

* It was raining **so** I stayed home.
* I was tired **so** I slept early.

---

## 6. IF (agar)

👉 condition (shart)

* **If** you study, you will pass.
* **If** it rains, we will stay home.

---

# ⚠️ Important Correction

👉 tum ne "between" likha tha
❌ between = preposition (conjunction nahi)

👉 Example:

* between Ali and Ahmed (ye conjunction nahi)

---

# 🧠 Easy Trick

👉 Question poocho:

* 2 sentences jorne hain? → conjunction
* reason batana hai? → because
* result batana hai? → so
* choice? → or

---

# 🔥 Final Simple Line

👉 **Conjunction = words jo sentences ko jorte hain**

---

# 6. 📘 Interjection kya hota hai?

👉 **Interjection = wo words jo hum apni sudden feelings (emotion) express karne ke liye use karte hain**

---

# 🧠 Simple samajh:

👉 Jab hum **achanak khushi, dukh, surprise ya pain** feel karte hain
👉 to hum jo words bolte hain → **interjections**

---

# 😲 Examples (Feelings ke sath)

## 😊 Khushi (Happiness)

* Wow!
* Hurray!

👉 Example:

* Wow! This is amazing!
* Hurray! We won the match!

---

## 😲 Surprise

* Oh!
* Ah!

👉 Example:

* Oh! I didn’t know that.
* Ah! What a big house!

---

## 😖 Pain / Sadness

* Ouch!
* Uff!

👉 Example:

* Ouch! It hurts.
* Uff! I am tired.

---

## 😄 Laughter

* Haha!
* Hahaha!

👉 Example:

* Haha! That was funny.

---

# ⚠️ Important Corrections (tumhari list)

❌ alias → galat (ye interjection nahi hai)

✔️ Better learning words:

* wow
* hurray
* oh
* ah
* ouch
* uff
* haha

# 🧠 Easy Trick

👉 Interjection = **feelings ka short expression**

# 🔥 Final Simple Line

👉 **Interjection = sudden emotion express karne wale words**

# 7.  Preposition kya hota hai?

👉 **Preposition = wo chhota word jo kisi cheez ki position (jagah), time ya relation batata hai**

---

# 🧠 Simple samajh:

👉 Preposition batata hai:

* 📍 **kahan? (place)**
* ⏰ **kab? (time)**
* 🔗 **kis ke sath / relation**

---

# 📍 1. Place (jagah)

👉 cheez kahan hai?

* The book is **on** the table.
  👉 kitab table **par** hai

* The cat is **under** the chair.
  👉 billi kursi **ke neeche** hai

* He is **in** the room.
  👉 wo room **mein** hai

---

# ⏰ 2. Time (waqt)

* I wake up **at** 7 AM
  👉 main 7 baje uthta hoon

* I study **in** the morning
  👉 main subah parhta hoon

* I will come **on** Monday
  👉 main Monday ko aunga

---

# 🔗 3. Relation (taluk)

* I am sitting **with** my friend
  👉 main dost ke **sath** hoon

* This gift is **for** you
  👉 ye gift tumhare **liye** hai

---

# 🔥 Common Prepositions

👉 in, on, at, under, over, with, for, to, from

---

# 🧠 Easy Trick

👉 Preposition = **jagah + time + relation batane wala word**

---

# 🎯 Final Simple Line

👉 **Preposition wo word hota hai jo batata hai ke koi cheez kahan hai, kab hai, ya kis ke sath hai**

# 7. Affect vs Effect (Super Simple)

## 🟢 1. Affect (verb)

👉 **Affect = asar daalna (action)**
👉 Ye **kaam (verb)** hota hai

### Example:

* This smell **affects** my health.
  👉 (ye meri health par asar daal rahi hai)

---

## 🔵 2. Effect (noun)

👉 **Effect = result / nateeja**
👉 Ye **cheez (noun)** hota hai

### Example:

* The **effect** of dirty water is disease.
  👉 (gande pani ka nateeja bimari hai)

---

# 🧠 Easy Trick (Yaad rakhne ka)

👉 **Affect = Action (dono A se)**
👉 **Effect = End result (E = End)**

---

# 🔥 Compare (side by side)

* Pollution **affects** people.
  👉 (asar daalta hai)

* The **effect** of pollution is illness.
  👉 (nateeja)

---

# ⚡ Tumhari mistake samjho

❌ "which effect the health"
👉 galat

✔️ "which **affects** the health"
👉 sahi (kyun ke yahan action ho raha hai)

---

# 🎯 Final Simple Line

👉 **Affect = asar daalna (verb)**
👉 **Effect = nateeja (noun)**

# 8. Drainage ka matlab

👉 **Drainage = pani nikalne ka system**

📌 Urdu:
👉 **پانی نکالنے کا نظام**
👉 ya **نکاسی آب کا نظام**

---

# 🧠 Simple samajh:

👉 Jab barsaat ya gandha pani jama ho jata hai
👉 usay nikalne ke liye jo system hota hai
👉 usay **drainage system** kehte hain

---

# 🔧 Real Life Example

* Roads ke side jo **naliyaan (drains)** hoti hain
  👉 ye sab drainage system ka part hoti hain

---

# 🧾 Example Sentences

* The drainage system is not working.
  👉 drainage system kaam nahi kar raha

* There is no proper drainage in our area.
  👉 hamare area mein sahi drainage system nahi hai

---

# ⚡ Easy Trick

👉 **Drainage = pani ko bahar nikalna**

---

# 🎯 Final Simple Line

👉 **Drainage = pani ko jama hone se bachane aur bahar nikalne ka system**

## in , at , on:
Great 👍 chalo **in / on / at** ko bohat simple tareeke se samjhte hain:

---

# 📘 1. IN (andar / andar ke andar)

👉 jab koi cheez **andar ho**
👉 ya **bada time period** ho

### 📍 Place:

* The book is **in** the bag
  👉 kitab bag **ke andar** hai

### ⏰ Time:

* I study **in** the morning
  👉 main subah parhta hoon

* I was born **in** 2005
  👉 main 2005 mein paida hua

---

# 📘 2. ON (upar / surface par)

👉 jab koi cheez **kisi surface (upar)** ho

### 📍 Place:

* The book is **on** the table
  👉 kitab table **par** hai

### ⏰ Time:

* I have a class **on** Monday
  👉 meri class Monday ko hai

---

# 📘 3. AT (exact point / specific)

👉 jab **exact jagah ya exact time** ho

### 📍 Place:

* I am **at** home
  👉 main ghar par hoon

* She is **at** the bus stop
  👉 wo bus stop par hai

### ⏰ Time:

* I wake up **at** 7 AM
  👉 main 7 baje uthta hoon

---

# 🧠 Easy Trick (Golden Rule)

👉 **IN → andar / bada time**
👉 **ON → upar / days**
👉 **AT → exact point**

---

# 🔥 Quick Compare

* in the room → room ke andar
* on the table → table ke upar
* at the door → darwaze par

---

# 🎯 Final Simple Line

👉 **in = andar**
👉 **on = upar**
👉 **at = exact point**
