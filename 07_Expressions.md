## **Communication & Daily Talk:**

**1. Call it a day**
* **Meaning:** Kaam ko rok dena ya bas kar dena (Matlab aaj ke liye itna hi kaam kaafi hai).
* **Improved Sentence:** After studying for 4 hours at night, I feel tired and decide to **call it a day** and go to bed.

**2. Catch someone’s eye**
* **Meaning:** Kisi ki tawajjo (attention) hasil karna.
* **Improved Sentence:** During the Zoom meeting, I used Excalidraw to draw the architecture diagrams of our project because I wanted to **catch the team’s eye**.

**3. Give someone a hand**
* **Meaning:** Kisi ki madad karna ya hath batana.
* **Improved Sentence:** Whenever junior developers or classmates need help with a project or topic, I always **give them a hand**.

**4. Keep an eye on**
* **Meaning:** Ghaur se dekhna ya nazar rakhna.
* **Improved Sentence:** While batting, I always **keep an eye on** the ball very carefully to hit a six.

**5. Take it easy**
* **Meaning:** Relax hona, sukoon karna, ya kisi cheez ko asaan lena.
* **Improved Sentence:** After a long study session, I like to sit on the sofa and **take it easy** for a few minutes.
